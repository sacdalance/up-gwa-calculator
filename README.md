# UP GWA Calculator and Tracker

**Free GWA calculator for University of the Philippines students.**

🔗 **[upgwa.vercel.app](https://upgwa.vercel.app)**

---

## What it does

- **Compute your GWA** — enter your grades and units per subject, get your weighted average instantly
- **Track Latin Honors** — see if you're on track for Summa Cum Laude (≤1.20), Magna Cum Laude (≤1.45), or Cum Laude (≤1.75)
- **Check Scholar Eligibility** — University Scholar (≤1.45) and College Scholar (≤1.75) per semester
- **Project your GWA** — see what grades you need in remaining units to hit your target honor
- **Import from AMIS** — paste your grades directly from the UP AMIS portal, no manual entry needed
- **What-if scenarios** — simulate different grade outcomes before finals

## Who it's for

UP students from any campus — UP Diliman, UPLB, UP Baguio, UP Manila, and more.

## How Latin Honors work in UP

| Honor | Cumulative GWA |
|---|---|
| Summa Cum Laude | ≤ 1.20 |
| Magna Cum Laude | ≤ 1.45 |
| Cum Laude | ≤ 1.75 |

## How Scholar Eligibility works

| Status | Semester GWA | Minimum Units |
|---|---|---|
| University Scholar | ≤ 1.45 | 15 units |
| College Scholar | ≤ 1.75 | 15 units |

No grade must exceed 3.00.

## Privacy

No sign-up. No data collected. Everything runs in your browser.
