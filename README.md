# UP GWA Calculator and Tracker

**Free GWA calculator for University of the Philippines students.**

Link: **https://upgwa.vercel.app** | **https://ginagwa-mo-web.vercel.app**

---

## Original Work

GinaGWA mo?! was independently designed and developed by **Lance Gabriel Sacdalan** since March 2026, with feedback and suggestions from friends, fellow interns, and the online community. All features are original works. At the time of release, no other UP GWA tool offered these capabilities.

- GWA calculator and cumulative tracker
- Latin Honors projection (Summa, Magna, Cum Laude)
- University and College Scholar eligibility
- What-If GWA projector
- Paste import optimized for AMIS
- Screenshot scan via on-device OCR
- Export and import JSON backup
- Share GWA card as PNG for Instagram stories
- Grade trend analysis
- Auto-sort terms chronologically
- Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop

## How Latin Honors work in UP

| Honor | Cumulative GWA |
|---|---|
| Summa Cum Laude | <= 1.20 |
| Magna Cum Laude | <= 1.45 |
| Cum Laude | <= 1.75 |

## How Scholar Eligibility works

| Status | Semester GWA | Minimum Units |
|---|---|---|
| University Scholar | <= 1.45 | 15 units |
| College Scholar | <= 1.75 | 15 units |

No grade must exceed 3.00.

## Privacy

Your academic data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server. This application uses Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregate visit statistics - no personal information is involved.

All data you enter is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's built-in localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

If you use the screenshot scan feature, your image is processed entirely on your device using Tesseract OCR. No image data is ever transmitted, stored, or shared.

No sign-up. No data collected. Everything runs in your browser. Your grades never leave your device.

## Accuracy Disclaimer

All computations are unofficial estimates for informational and planning purposes only. Results may not reflect incomplete grades (INC), dropped subjects (DRP), or other academic standing adjustments. Always verify your official academic records with the Office of the University Registrar (OUR).
